# Domain Name Ideas

## Top Picks
1. **chickencoopguide.com** - Direct, SEO-friendly, buyer intent
2. **thechickenguide.com** - Clean, brandable, broad
3. **chickensimplified.com** - Beginner-friendly positioning
4. **flockguide.com** - Short, memorable, niche
5. **happyhenhq.com** - Friendly vibe, alliterative
6. **firstflock.com** - Beginner angle, brandable
7. **chickenkeeper101.com** - Matches ShrimpTank101 naming pattern
8. **backyardflockguide.com** - Descriptive, keyword-rich
9. **henhousehq.com** - Catchy, alliterative
10. **eggandhen.com** - Simple, memorable

## Notes
- Avoid anything with "backyard chickens" exactly (backyardchickens.com is the big forum)
- Shorter is better for branding
- Keyword in domain still helps slightly for SEO
- .com only (no .io, .co, etc. for affiliate sites)
